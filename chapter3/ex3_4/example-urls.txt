Example URLs:
Default parameters:
http://localhost:8000/

Custom size:
http://localhost:8000/?width=800&height=600

Custom cells (resolution):
http://localhost:8000/?cells=50

http://localhost:8000/?cells=150

Custom xyrange (zooming):
http://localhost:8000/?xyrange=15.0 (zoomed in)

http://localhost:8000/?xyrange=60.0 (zoomed out)

Custom colors:
http://localhost:8000/?color=red

http://localhost:8000/?color=blue

http://localhost:8000/?color=green

http://localhost:8000/?color=height (height-based gradient - default)

http://localhost:8000/?color=%23ff00ff (hex color code)

Combined parameters:
http://localhost:8000/?width=800&height=600&cells=120&xyrange=40&color=orange
